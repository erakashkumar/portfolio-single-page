Who I Am

About Me

IT professional, healthcare tech specialist, and digital growth strategist with 5+ years of hands-on experience across government and private healthcare institutions.

I'm Akash Kumar Mishra, a B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering graduate from DRIEMS University, Cuttack. My career has been defined by a singular focus — using technology to improve how healthcare is delivered and managed.

My journey began at SCB Medical College & Hospital, Cuttack — one of India's largest government hospitals — where I spent 3.5 years managing web infrastructure, IT assets, HIMS modules, and large-scale hospital events. This foundation built my appreciation for precision, reliability, and the critical nature of healthcare IT.

At UNCE Hospital, Bhubaneswar, I took on my most comprehensive challenge: leading a complete end-to-end hospital digitization project. From Billing and OPD to OT workflows, Aadhaar/ABHA integration, and TPA/BSKY portals — I implemented every module and ensured seamless patient data flow from entry to discharge.

My time at PMSSH, Bhubaneswar showed me the power of digital marketing in healthcare. Starting from zero social presence, I grew organic reach to over 80,000 impressions — without spending a single rupee on ads — through strategic content, SEO, and patient testimonial videos.

Today at Cognizant, I'm part of Google's content moderation ecosystem — working on I2V, Videogen, and Autotrim AI pipelines, ensuring quality and policy compliance at massive scale.
